ABOUT ALFRED GAMES

One human + AI copilots

Alfred Games is a tiny indie studio powered by a single developer, amplified by modern AI tooling. The goal isn’t to chase trends, but to build tight arcade loops, expressive visuals, and experimental ideas that would usually require a much larger team.

From art direction, to code reviews, to rapid prototyping inside Unity, AI is treated as a collaborator. That means faster iteration, more time spent tuning feel and flow, and the freedom to explore weird concepts without months of overhead.

Pillars

  • Gameplay first, visuals second
  • Short, replayable sessions
  • Transparent dev process

Toolchain

  • Unity + C#
  • Generative art pipelines
  • LLM-assisted design + tuning

Studio Log

Every project starts from a simple question: “Would this be fun to pick up for five minutes?” The rest of the design is built around that core. Systems, visuals, and audio all exist to serve the loop.

Coin Catcher 2D is the first public experiment in this direction, with more prototypes, devlogs, and behind-the-scenes breakdowns planned as the Alfred Games catalogue grows.
